# Example run — Curriculum mode

> Inputs used: **BJJ · white belt · 3 days/week · 4 weeks (pre-grading block)**
> Syllabus pasted in: mount/side-control/back/guard/half-guard positions, mount + side-control escapes, RNC, cross-collar choke, armbar & triangle from guard, kimura, scissor + hip-bump sweep, knee-slice + torreando pass, double & single leg, shrimping/posture/base fundamentals.

## 4-Week White Belt Curriculum

**Week 1 — Survive & Escape (the foundation)**
- Focus: never get stuck. Frames, hip movement, escapes before offense.
- Techniques: shrimping/bridging fundamentals, mount escape (elbow-knee + bridge & roll), side-control escape (frame to knee-shield).
- Day 1: shrimping + bridging mechanics → mount escape (elbow-knee). Day 2: mount escape (bridge & roll) + when to pick which. Day 3: side-control escape to knee-shield, then recover guard.
- Review: none yet (foundation week).

**Week 2 — Guard Retention & First Sweeps**
- Focus: keep guard, then start reversing.
- Techniques: closed-guard posture/grips, scissor sweep, hip-bump sweep.
- Day 1: closed-guard grips + breaking posture → scissor sweep. Day 2: hip-bump sweep + chaining it off a failed scissor. Day 3: live guard-retention rounds (positional, no subs).
- Review: revisit Week 1 escapes as the "reset" when guard is lost.

**Week 3 — First Submissions from Guard**
- Focus: finish from the position you now hold.
- Techniques: cross-collar choke, armbar from guard, triangle from guard.
- Day 1: cross-collar choke (grip depth + elbow cue). Day 2: armbar from guard + the failed-armbar → triangle transition. Day 3: triangle finish details + angle.
- Review: sweeps from Week 2 (sweep → consolidate → submit).

**Week 4 — Passing, Takedowns & Grading Sharpening**
- Focus: top game + tie the whole syllabus together.
- Techniques: knee-slice pass, torreando pass, double leg, single leg.
- Day 1: knee-slice + torreando passing. Day 2: double & single leg entries (safe, controlled). Day 3: full grading dry-run — flow through escapes → guard → sweep → submit → pass.
- Review: spaced repetition of Weeks 1–3, light positional rounds.

## Grading-Readiness Check (most-missed at white belt)
1. Bridge-and-roll mount escape under real pressure (people freeze and just hold).
2. Triangle angle — finishing straight-on instead of cutting the angle.
3. Cross-collar grip too shallow to actually choke.
4. Standing: a safe takedown entry without diving headfirst.
